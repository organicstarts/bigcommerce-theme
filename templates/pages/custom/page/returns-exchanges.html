Edible Goods and Hygenic Products

We are unable to accept any returns or exchanges on perishable (edible) goods and hygenic products such as baby care products sold at Organic Start.

The products that we offer on our website are considered highly sensitive as they are either fed or used on babies of all ages. Inorder to protect the integrity of our inventory, returns and exchanges are not permitted for these products.

Clothes and Accessories

Parents may return or exchange clothes, accessories or Powder Nests up to 14 days after recieving the product. We are unable to accept any returns or exchanges for any products after 14 days.

Claims for products damaged during transit are also accepted up to 3 days after recieving an order.
